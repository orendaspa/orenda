About Orenda Spa

A Sanctuary Where Beauty Meets Inner Harmony

At “Orenda Spa”, we believe true radiance begins within. Founded by Banyan Xu—a licensed esthetician and former MBA professional—our Maryland-based sanctuary blends “science-backed skincare” with “holistic wellness” to help you look and feel your most confident.

Our Story: From Boardroom to Treatment Room

After years in the corporate world, Banyan followed a calling to redefine beauty—one that nurtures “both skin and soul”. Combining a business mindset with advanced esthetics training, they created Orenda Spa as a place where “expertise meets empathy”, and every treatment is designed to “restore, uplift, and empower”.

The name “Orenda” (from the Iroquoian word for “spiritual energy”) reflects our philosophy: “Beauty is more than skin-deep”. Here, you’ll find curated rituals that harmonize outer glow with inner peace—because when you feel balanced, your confidence shines.
