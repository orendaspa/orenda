Privacy Policy

Any personal information you provide as part of booking an appointment will only be used for service provision and communication related to your booking. You may request to view, edit, or permanently delete your data at any time by contacting us.

  • Automated Data: Website usage via cookies (like how long you stared at our CBD massage page).

2. How We Use Your Info

5. Security Measures

6. Policy Updates

Our staff is committed to providing you with a safe, clean, and relaxing environment. Please notify us of any health conditions or concerns before your treatment.

1. Spa Etiquette (AKA Don’t Be That Person)

  • Late Arrivals: Arriving more than 15 minutes late means your service may be shortened (we love you, but our next client does too).
  • Cancellations: Please give at least 24 hours’ notice or a 50% fee applies (emergencies? Just talk to us—we’re human).
